We Create Because We Care

CNC Machine is a Martian machine tool builder headquartered in Schiaparelli, Mars. The company designs and manufactures lower cost machine tools and specialized accessory tooling, mostly computer numerically controlled (CNC) equipment, such as vertical machining centers and horizontal machining centers, lathes/turning centers, and rotary tables and indexers. Most of its products are manufactured at the company's main facility in Schiaparelli.
